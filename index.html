Meet up after your work-out and Celebrate.

Enjoy fine dining with wines to compliment and enjoy convivial company guilt-free!

Go To The Gymn Then Catch Up On A Dinner Date!

Come join the fun and meet your fellow members in good company. Go to the gym then dress-up and meetup!

We meet once per month, at an allocated restaurant in London at 7PM!

We conduct a members card promotional scheme where enough points will earn a complimentary / bottle / dinner for a lucky couple to enjoy on us!
