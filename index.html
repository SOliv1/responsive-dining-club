Jules Fitness Flight Dining Club

Meet up after your work-out and Celebrate.

Enjoy fine dining with wines to compliment and enjoy convivial company guilt-free!

Jules Flight Dining Club is a free MeetUp group sponsored by Joules Flight Club.

"I look forward to our once per month fine dining experience. I can exercise, then look forward to a convivial evening, meeting new people and catching up with friends every month in a fabulous restaurant!"

-John L. - Accountant/frequent gym and fitness member since 2016



"We look forward to our monthly dinners where we earn points for our dinner dates. Fitness is Fun but we always enjoy Good Food and Great Company"

-James and Lucy P. - Bankers

Come join the fun and meet your fellow members in good company.

We meet once per month, at an allocated restaurant in London at 7PM!

We conduct a members card promotional scheme where enough points will earn a complimentary / bottle / dinner for a lucky couple to enjoy dinner on us!

Frequent-Flight-Dining Card points gets a complimentary drink; bottle of wine or perhaps a one course dinner with the compliments of Jules Fitness and fine dining experience.
